---
title: Categories
description: Organizing your novel library with custom taxonomy.
---

As your collection expands, the default unified library view may become difficult to navigate. Novon provides a robust **Categories** system to help you segment your novels by status, genre, or personal preference.

## Creating Categories

1. Navigate to the **Library** tab.
2. Tap the <Icon name="Settings" /> filter icon in the top right to reveal the Library Management sheet.
3. Select **Edit Categories**.
4. Tap the `+` button to define a new category, such as "Currently Reading," "Completed," or "On Hold."

<Callout type="info" title="Persistance">
You can reorder categories by dragging the handle icon. This order dictates the sequence of the category tabs displayed at the top of your library interface.
</Callout>

## Assigning Novels

To link a novel to one or more categories:

1. Perform a **Long-press** on a novel cover within your library to enter Selection Mode.
2. Select all novels you wish to categorize.
3. Tap the **Tag** icon on the bottom action bar.
4. Select the desired categories from the list and tap Save.

<Callout type="tip" title="Multi-Categorization">
Novon allows a single novel to reside in multiple categories simultaneously. For example, a title can appear in both "Action" and "Finished" categories.
</Callout>
