Novon is an open-source, offline-first novel reader built using the Flutter framework, currently in **Early {{APP_STATUS}} (v{{APP_VERSION}})**. It consolidates webnovels, light novels, and serialized fiction from hundreds of different sources into a single reading experience.

Unlike traditional novel apps that rely on central servers, Novon is decentralized. It uses **Extensions**—small JavaScript-based plug-ins maintained by the community—to extract text and chapters directly from the original websites.

<Callout type="info" title="Privacy First">
Your reading history, library, and settings stay on your device. Novon does not host content on its own servers.
</Callout>

## <Icon name="Download" /> Installation

Novon is available for Android, iOS, Windows, macOS, and Linux.

1. Navigate to the [Download Page](/download) to grab the installer for your platform.
2. For Android users, download the `.apk` file and install it manually.
3. Upon first launch, the Onboarding Flow will guide you through selecting a storage location and preferred languages.

## <Icon name="Zap" /> Adding Extensions

By default, Novon is a blank slate. To read novels, you must install extensions.

1. Tap the **Browse** tab at the bottom of the navigation bar.
2. Switch to the **Extensions** sub-tab at the top.
3. Browse the list of community extensions. Tap the **Download** icon next to any source you want to read from, such as Royal Road or Wuxiaworld.

<Callout type="warning" title="Security Warning">
Only install extensions from trusted developers. Extensions execute JavaScript logic on your device to parse websites.
</Callout>

### <Icon name="Settings" /> Custom Repositories

To access third-party or specialized extensions, you can add custom repositories in the settings:

1. Tap <Icon name="Settings" /> **Settings** in the More tab.
2. Navigate to **Browse** and then **Extension Repositories**.
3. Tap the `+` button and paste the URL of the repository's `index.json` file.

## <Icon name="Zap" /> Building Your Library

Once extensions are installed, you can start building your collection:

1. Open the **Browse** tab and select an installed source.
2. Use the search bar or browse the "Popular" and "Latest" sections.
3. Tap on a novel to view its synopsis and data.
4. Tap the **Heart** icon to add it to your Library.

<Callout type="tip" title="Automatic Sync">
Novels in your library will automatically check for new chapters. You can customize the check frequency in the <Icon name="Settings" /> **Notifications** settings panel.
</Callout>

## <Icon name="BookOpen" /> The Reading Experience

The reader is designed for high-fidelity typography. Tap the center of the screen while reading to access common controls:

* **Display Modes:** Toggle between infinite vertical scrolling, paged mode, or the borderless webtoon view.
* **Themes:** Select from Light, Dark, Sepia, or AMOLED Black.
* **Typography:** Modify font family, font size, and line height to suit your preference.
* **Auto-scroll:** In vertical scroll mode, you can enable a continuous hands-free scroll.
