### How do I change the font?
Tap the center of the screen to open the reader overlay, tap the <Icon name="BookOpen" /> typography settings icon, and select "Font Family". Novon includes high-quality fonts bundled for offline use:
* **Alexandria:** A modern, clean font optimized for legibility.
* **El Messiri:** A graceful serif style perfect for long-form reading.
* **Lalezar:** A bold, stylistic choice for a unique interface feel.

### What is AMOLED true black?
While the standard Dark theme utilizes a deep grey background to minimize smearing and eye strain, the **AMOLED True Black** mode sets the background hex code to `#000000`. This allows OLED displays to physically turn off individual pixels, significantly reducing battery consumption during reading sessions.

### How do I enable Auto-Scroll?
Auto-Scroll is exclusively available in the "Vertical Scroll" reading mode. To enable it:
1. Ensure your reading mode is set to **Vertical Scroll** in the <Icon name="BookOpen" /> settings.
2. Tap the center of the screen to reveal the overlay.
3. Tap the **Play** icon to begin the continuous scroll.
4. Use the slider to fine-tune the scroll speed to your reading pace.

<Callout type="tip" title="Hands-Free Reading">
You can also toggle **Volume Button Navigation** in settings to flip pages or scroll using the physical hardware buttons on your device.
</Callout>
