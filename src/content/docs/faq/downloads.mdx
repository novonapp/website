### Where are downloaded chapters stored?
Downloaded chapters are stored as raw text or HTML payloads inside your configured storage directory. By default, these are located in the `downloads/` folder within your Novon storage root.

<Callout type="info" title="Storage Configuration">
You can view or change your current storage root in <Icon name="Settings" /> **Data Settings > Storage Location**.
</Callout>

### Can I export downloaded chapters as EPUB or PDF?
Novon is primarily a specialized reader rather than an export utility. While we are investigating community interest in EPUB bundling, there is currently no official support for exporting local chapters to external formats.

### Downloads are stuck at pending
If your download queue is stalled, please verify the following:

1. **Active Connectivity:** Ensure you have a stable network connection.
2. **Wi-Fi Constraints:** Check if **Wi-Fi Only Downloads** is toggled on in your settings while you are using cellular data.
3. **Storage Health:** Ensure your device has sufficient remaining disk space and that Novon has write permissions for your selected storage folder.

<Callout type="warning" title="IP Bans">
Rapidly downloading thousands of chapters from a single source may trigger automated security blocks (like Cloudflare challenges) on the source website. Novon automatically implements rate-limiting to minimize this risk.
</Callout>
